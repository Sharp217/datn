## Coaching Decisions & Rationale

### ✅ What he did right
- Maintained 1-2 hour response cadence — matched her energy, didn't seem desperate
- Grabbed her hand in the crowd — confident, physical without being pushy
- Kissed her on the dance floor — took the shot
- Did NOT go in for a second kiss at the car — smart, left her wanting more
- Held the ticket reveal — didn't immediately tell her, saving it for a natural moment
- Gave her his Instagram when asked rather than pushing for hers first

### ⚠️ One thing to watch
- Sent 5 messages in a row at one point — consolidate into 1-2 max going forward to keep energy measured

### 🎯 Current Status

## Dante's Takeaways (Training Notes)

1. **Mirroring response cadence** is powerful — don't always reply instantly, match their pace
2. **Playful > thirsty** — the exclusive pics line worked because it was funny, not desperate
3. **Physical escalation at concerts** is natural — the environment does the work
4. **Pullback ≠ rejection** — she kissed back, the pullback was surprise, not disinterest
5. **Future anchors (shows, events) = green flags** — when she invites him to something, she's investing
6. **Hold your cards** — the ticket reveal is a perfect example of not showing everything at once
7. **Let her chase sometimes** — she messaged him first on Sunday, she's asking for his insta, she's flirting. Don't chase what's already coming toward you.
