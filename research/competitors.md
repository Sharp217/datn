# Dat'n — Competitor Research

_Last updated: 2026-03-25_

---

## Market Overview

The AI dating assistant space is real and growing. YourMove AI alone has 300,000+ users and has been featured in Washington Post, Forbes, and CBC. The market exists — nobody has won it yet with a truly differentiated product.

**The universal weakness across all competitors:** They are tools, not characters. You paste something in, you get something out. Zero personality, zero ongoing relationship, zero end-to-end experience.

---

## Competitors

### 1. YourMove AI — yourmove.ai ⭐ BIGGEST THREAT
- **Users:** 300,000+
- **Press:** Washington Post, Forbes, CBC
- **Features:**
  - Opener generator (paste profile → 3 openers)
  - Reply suggestions (paste message → 3 replies)
  - Profile writer (bio generation)
  - Profile review (data-driven feedback)
  - AI enhanced photos
- **Pricing:** Freemium, ~$9.99-19.99/mo
- **Positioning:** "Dating apps are exhausting" — reduce the work
- **Weakness:** Pure utility. No name, no personality, no memory, no character. Transactional.

### 2. Rizz — rizz.app
- Claims "#1 AI Dating Assistant"
- Went viral on TikTok
- Primarily opener and reply generation
- Mobile-first
- **Weakness:** Same as YourMove — tool not a character, no differentiation

### 3. Keys
- AI conversation coach
- Opener and reply focused
- Less traction than YourMove
- **Weakness:** Narrow feature set, no brand personality

### 4. Wingman
- Profile review and photo selection focused
- Less conversation coaching
- **Weakness:** Single use case, not a full concierge

### 5. Teaser AI
- Bio generation only
- Very narrow
- **Weakness:** One trick pony

### 6. AIMM
- AI matchmaker — focuses on finding compatible matches
- Different angle (matching vs. coaching)
- **Weakness:** Matchmaking is a harder problem, less immediate value

## Key Insight

YourMove has 300,000 users with a generic name and zero personality. That's proof the market is real and hungry. Dat'n can take significant market share by being the product people actually WANT to talk about — not just use.

**The positioning:** YourMove is a calculator. Dante is a wingman. People don't brag about using a calculator.

## GTM Implications
- **TikTok is the channel** — Rizz proved it, YourMove leveraged it
- Content angle: real results, real convos, Dante's personality shining through
- Don't compete on features — compete on character and relationship
